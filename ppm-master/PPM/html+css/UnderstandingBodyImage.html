Understanding Body Image

You've come to the right place if you want to know how you, or someone you know, feels and thinks about the way you appear. We'll discuss what body image is and how it might affect how we think, feel, and act in this series of tools. We'll also look into why we have body image issues and how we might start to have a more positive relationship with our bodies. There are no eating disorder labels, stereotypes, or criteria to be found here. This resource is for you, regardless of who you are or whether you have been diagnosed with an eating disorder.


Before you start

Asking for and accessing help can be scary.

By being here and reading this,

you’ve taken a brave step already.

If this all feels a little daunting

please know that our aim isn’t to

try and make you love your body.

Our hope is to simply help you reflect

on what’s going on inside.

So you can understand a little more about

how you’re feeling about your body and help

you cope a little better.

Causes

Visit Causes and Consequences

Many people who are overweight are

self-assured in their appearance and have

positive food interactions. Just by looking

at someone, we can't tell what their eating

patterns is like or how they eat.

Unfortunately, numerous larger people

who struggle with food and body

appearance are frequently offered

weight-loss techniques. This may

exacerbate the situation, as

modifying your body is a surface-level solution.
